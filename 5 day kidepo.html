5 Day Wildlife Safari to Kidepo Valley NP which is located on Uganda's border with Sudan and Kenya
in the extreme corner in the Northeast of the country. The park comprises rolling savannah grassland and
a rugged spectacular mountain landscape.

The park is accessible by road or by air from Entebbe Airport. Kidepois considered one of the wildest,
original African parks in Africa,offeringa great experience of nature and game driving
